TPEP Anti-Corrosion Steel Pipe

Excellent Anti-Corrosion Solution for Modern Pipeline Engineering

Core Product Features and Technological Innovation

TPEP anti-corrosion steel pipes achieve comprehensive upgrades in anti-corrosion performance through composite technology of outer wall three-layer polyethylene (3PE) and inner wall fusion-bonded epoxy resin (FBE). Compared to traditional single-layer anti-corrosion pipe materials, their anti-corrosion lifespan is improved by 3-5 times, demonstrating excellent performance in harsh environments such as high salinity and humidity. Using fourth-generation thermal fusion wrapping technology, they form steel-plastic alloy layers that combine impact resistance and chemical inertness.

Technical Parameters and Process Advantages

Structural Design

  • External Anti-corrosion Layer (3PE): Total thickness ≥4.2mm, compliant with GB/T23257-2009 standard.
  • Internal Anti-corrosion Layer (FBE): Food-grade epoxy powder, thickness ≥450μm, certified to CJ/T120-2008.
  • Hydraulic Characteristics: Roughness as low as 0.0081, reducing pump station energy consumption by 15%.

Production Process

  • Medium Frequency Heating Technology: Base pipe preheating 200-220℃, bonding strength ≥50MPa.
  • Repair-free Connection: DN600 and below use stainless steel lined ports to simplify construction.
