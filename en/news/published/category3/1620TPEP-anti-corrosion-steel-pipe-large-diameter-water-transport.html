TPEP Anti-Corrosion Steel Pipe

Excellence in High-Performance Pipeline Anti-Corrosion Technology

Product Overview

TPEP anti-corrosion steel pipe is a high-performance pipeline featuring dual protection technology with Three-Layer Polyethylene (3PE) and Fusion Bonded Epoxy (FBE) coatings, specifically designed for underground or overhead transportation in harsh environments. With its exceptional anti-corrosion performance and mechanical strength, it is widely used in petroleum, natural gas, chemical, and water supply industries, making it the ideal choice for long-distance transportation pipelines.

Three-Layer Composite Anti-Corrosion Structure

1

Fusion Bonded Epoxy (FBE)

Applied directly to the steel pipe surface, providing excellent adhesion, chemical corrosion resistance and anti-cathodic disbondment properties. Thickness typically ranges from 150-400 microns.

2

Adhesive Layer (AD)

Enhances bonding between FBE and polyethylene layers, ensuring overall integrity of the anti-corrosion coating. Thickness generally ranges from 170-250 microns.

3

Polyethylene Layer (PE)

Provides mechanical protection and waterproofing performance with high impact resistance and wear resistance. Thickness ranges from 1.5-3 millimeters according to requirements.

Construction & Maintenance

Construction Guidelines

Must strictly follow specifications to ensure anti-corrosion layer remains intact during transportation and installation, with special attention to joint treatment in welding areas.

Maintenance Recommendations

After pipeline commissioning, establish regular inspection schedules, assess anti-corrosion layer condition through detection equipment, and perform timely, professional repairs on any identified damage.
