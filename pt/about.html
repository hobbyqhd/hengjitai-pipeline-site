Company Profile

Steady Growth, Innovative Development

Cangzhou Hengjitai Pipeline Equipment Co., Ltd. is located in Yanshan County Economic Development Zone, Hebei Province. We are a pipeline equipment supplier focusing on plastic-coated steel pipes and pipeline anti-corrosion field. Founded in 2000, after more than twenty years of development, we have become an influential enterprise in the industry.

Hengjitai has a modern production base equipped with advanced automated production lines and testing equipment, with an annual production capacity of 100,000 tons. We have passed ISO 9001 quality management system, ISO 14001 environmental management system and ISO 45001 occupational health and safety management system certifications, and obtained various qualifications including mining safety certificates and sanitary licensing.

As an industry pioneer, we continuously focus on technological innovation and product development, with multiple patented technologies in coating materials and anti-corrosion processes. Hengjitai products have been widely used in key engineering projects across the country and exported to multiple countries and regions overseas, winning unanimous praise from customers.

Factory & Laboratory

Modern Production Base, Professional Testing Equipment

Hengjitai owns a modern industrial park covering an area of 50,000 square meters, equipped with advanced automated production equipment and intelligent management systems. Our production lines include automatic coating production lines, steel pipe processing lines, testing and packaging lines, ensuring stable and reliable product quality.

The company's laboratory is equipped with comprehensive testing equipment, including coating adhesion testers, coating thickness gauges, impact testers, bending test equipment, tensile test equipment, etc., which can perform comprehensive physical and chemical property tests on products to ensure each batch meets relevant standard requirements.
