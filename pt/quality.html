Quality Management System

Comprehensive Quality Control Process to Ensure Every Product Meets Standards

Cangzhou Hengjitai Pipeline Equipment Co., Ltd. strictly implements the ISO 9001:2015 quality management system, establishing a comprehensive quality control system from raw material procurement to manufacturing, quality testing, and warehousing. Our quality control department operates independently, equipped with professional technical personnel and advanced testing equipment to ensure every batch of products meets national standards and industry specifications.

Our quality control system mainly includes the following aspects:

  • Raw Material Quality Control

    Strict inspection of all incoming materials to ensure they meet design requirements, establishing qualified supplier management system

  • Production Process Control

    Checkpoints at key processes throughout production, real-time monitoring of manufacturing parameters to ensure consistent product quality

  • Finished Product Inspection

    Comprehensive inspection of each batch of products, including appearance, dimensions, coating thickness, adhesion, mechanical properties, etc.

  • Quality Tracking System

    Product batch management and quality traceability mechanism, with each batch traceable back to raw material lots and production processes

Quality Policy

We adhere to the quality policy of "Quality First, Customer First, Continuous Innovation, Pursuit of Excellence" to provide customers with high-quality, reliable pipeline products and professional technical services.
